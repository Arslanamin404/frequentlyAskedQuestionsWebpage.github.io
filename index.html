Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

What is the return Policy?
The user can only return products within 3 days soon after the delivery of product.The amount of money to the user will be refunded in users bank account soon after the product is received by delivery boy.The receipt of product is mandatory in case of loss of receipt product will not returned and no refund will be entertained.
When will I receive my order?
We aim to ensure that you receive your order as quickly as possible.
Our average delivery time is approximately 7-10 business days, once we ship the products from the warehouse. Delivery times may vary a bit depending on customer location, any public holidays, and unforeseen circumstances.
Please note that our standard delivery times are estimates and may vary due to external factors. Packages are not shipped nor delivered on weekends and local public holidays. .
I haven’t received my package yet what do I do?
If you have received your Tracking Number, we urge you to directly contact your local Shipping Office. Shipping will have more accurate information on the status of the delivery.
If you have not received your tracking number, please contact us and we will look into this for you, immediately.
How do I track my order?
After successfully placing the order, you will receive a notification from our shipping partner containing your package’s tracking information and expected delivery date. The delivery can take up to 7-10 business days, once the order gets dispatched from the warehouse.

To track your package, simply use the tracking number received by email provided on the courier company's official website.
I have received the wrong product/size what do I do?
If you receive your package and it is not what you ordered, please contact us immediately and provide us with the following: Picture of the item(s) you received together with the delivery note (Please make sure that the details are clear and visible).

If a watch was ordered, please provide a picture a clear of the back of the watch.

If a ring was ordered, please provide a clear picture of the serial number found in the inner part of the ring.

We will do our best to assist you further. One of our representatives will get back to you as soon as possible within 1-2 business days. In the meantime, we suggest you to refer our sizing guide.
